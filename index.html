About Us

We provide holiday package & Tour operator specialized in Perhentian, Redang, Lang Tengah, Light House, Rawa & Susu Dara Island and many more.

We also offer cheap tour packages either domestic (within Malaysia) or international tours. Just tell us about the trip you’re dreaming of taking—they will make it a reality. Perhentian Setia Travel & Tours Sdn. Bhd. team consist of a group of travel expert which are strong emphasis on service quality, customer care, attentive listening and prompt response to enquiries within 24 hours.

We are a company with diverse talent & skills. Our leadership structure offers a dynamic atmosphere in which telented, creative & motivated people thrive. We don’t just acknowledge hard work & achievements, we reward it & groom it.

We have an experienced team and ambitious, vibrant, young professionals having ability to update with latest trends & requirements of our client.

Our team’s passion is to take challenges and to deliver to clients expectations.
